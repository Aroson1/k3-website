# About K³

K³ is an offline national-level competitive programming contest, hosted on Codeforces, designed to identify and reward top algorithmic talent.

* 40 teams from IIIT Kottayam
* 120+ teams from outside
* ₹1.5+ lakh prize pool

# Host Institute — IIIT Kottayam

IIIT Kottayam is an Institute of National Importance established by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

Students are admitted through the highly competitive JEE Main process, creating a campus of:

* Competitive programmers & ICPC participants
* Hackathon winners & developers
* Future software engineers and researchers

Partnering with IIIT Kottayam means direct visibility among skilled, job-ready engineers and top technical talent.
